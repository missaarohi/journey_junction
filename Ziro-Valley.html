Welcome to the Ziro-Valley

The valley gets its name from the village of Ziro, the last outpost before the international border with China. It is known for stunning natural landscapes that are a feast for the eyes. Surrounded by lush green hills and dense forests, the valley is a sanctuary for a wide variety of flora and fauna.
