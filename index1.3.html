Experience


Aaron Davis

In the heart of Mumbai for a week, every day brought a new adventure. From the iconic Gateway of India to the historic Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus, the city's rich past unfolded. A day immersed in Bollywood's magic at Film City was a highlight, offering a glimpse behind the scenes. The serenity of Marine Drive and the vibrant chaos of Chowpatty Beach provided a perfect balance. Mumbai's street food was a sensory delight, with flavors ranging from spicy pav bhaji to the simplicity of vada pav. A cultural immersion tour through Dharavi and the artistic vibrancy of Kala Ghoda showcased the city's diverse soul. The last day was dedicated to exploring bustling markets, bringing a perfect end to a week filled with memories in the city of dreams..





Carrie

Embarking on a mesmerizing journey through Rajasthan for a week, every day felt like a chapter from a fairy tale. The majestic Amer Fort in Jaipur left me in awe, a gateway to the state's regal history. The "Blue City" of Jodhpur painted a vibrant picture, with the towering Mehrangarh Fort dominating the skyline. In Udaipur, the serene Lake Pichola and the opulent City Palace created a romantic backdrop. Pushkar's spiritual aura during a visit to the Brahma Temple was a tranquil escape. Exploring the golden dunes of Jaisalmer and a night in a desert camp were surreal experiences. The markets of Jaipur and Jodhpur added a colorful touch to my Rajasthan odyssey, making it a week filled with royal grandeur and cultural richness.
